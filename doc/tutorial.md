# Tutorial Erglytics
## Overview
Welcome to Erglytics! This is a tutorial that will walk new users through the main workflow of our project at its current stage: to create an account, record a workout, review their workout, and join a team.

<br>By the end of the tutorial, a user should be able to accomplish these tasks by themself:
- Create their account
- Start a workout recording session
- Review their workout summary
- View their workout snapshots
- Join or create a team

Everything in this tutorial is very simple to follow, so that those without techncial experience can enjoy our project.
## Prerequisites
Before you begin using our project, you must have the following:
- A device with a webcam (phone preferred, but laptops or other devices work)
- Internet access
- Access to the Erglytics web application link
- A rowing machine
- Enough space to position the device so that you are in view on the rowing machine (in a side view)
- Preferred to have good lighting and a stable surface to have your device on
## Setup/Installation
1. Open the Erglytics application in your web browser using the shared link.
2. Select *Create Account*.
3. Enter a valid email and password.
4. Confirm your account's email through verification steps.

After your email is verified, you will be automatically taken to the home dashboard.
## First Workflow (step-by-step guide)
**1. Open the Capture Workout Page** <br>
From the home dashboard, select *Get Started* button on the *Capture Workout* card. This is where you'll be recording your workout.<br>
**2. Put yourself into position** <br>
Before you select *Start*, you need to get into correct position. Set up the device so that the view of the camera will be a side view of your body on the rowing machine. Try to ensure that your whole body will remain in frame.<br>
**3. Select start** <br>
Select *Start*, and then look at the guidance on the screen to ensure that your body is in the right position. It will inform you when you're in the right position, and will tell you to complete 3 strokes. After those 3 strokes, continue to erg for at least 5 seconds, so that it can record a 5 second clip of your workout. You can continue to workout after this if you'd like, but it's not necessary for the point of the tutorial.<br>
**4. Stop Recording** <br>
Once you're done, select *Stop*. You can then return to the home dashboard by selecting the home button in the top navigation bar.<br>
**5. View Workout Summary** <br>
Select the *Get Started* button on the *Workout Summaries* card. You'll see a singlular description card of your most recent workout. When you have more, they will fill up the screen in a grid. You can see the current info that we get out of your workout here, including a score of how well you were aligned.<br>
**6. View Snapshot**<br>
Return to the home dashboard by clicking the home button again. Select the *Get Started* button on the *Snapshot Library* card. Here you will be able to see a grid of clips recorded from your workout (depending on how long your workout was, and how many 5 second clips were taken). The most recent one will be at the top, where you can view how well your form looked after the fact.<br>
**7. Join a Team**<br>
Return to the home dashboard by clicking the home button again. Select the *Get Started* button on the *Team View* card. Unless you know someone else using the application that has a team already, you are going to have to create your own team. Create a Team Name, and put it into the text box. Then select *Create team*.<br>
**8. View Team**<br>
You can now see the team info, including the members. You can share the team ID with others, so that they can join this team, and you will be able to see them on the members list. If needed, you're able to leave the team by selecting *Leave team* at the top.<br>
## Expected Results
After completing the tutorial, the user should have been able to:
- Successfully create and verify an account
- Record a workout
- Access workout summary/details
- View a snapshot recorded from the workout
- Join and view a team

Successful results look like being able to complete each step, and seeing the resulting info each time (ie. workout summary is in the library, a snapshot exists, a team is created and viewable, etc.).
## Troubleshooting
**Camera Not Working**
- Refresh the page
- Allow camera permissions in the browser
- Try a different device

**Workout Summary isn't Produced**
- Try re-recording the workout and ensure that all messages are green when recording
- Refresh the page
- Log out and back in

**Login Issues**
- Confirm that you used the correct email/password
- Reset password if needed
