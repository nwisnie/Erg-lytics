# HOW-TO GUIDE

## How to Use Capture Workout

Users can `Capture Workout` create workouts for Erglytics. Press `Start` to start a recording session. Erglytics will not start capturing workout data until the body is in full frame and the body is in a side profile such as demonstarted in the image below. Erglytics uses green dots to track each individual landmark on a body. Pressing `Stop` will stop the workout. Snapshots of the workout can then be seen in `Snapshot Library`. Analysis of the workout can be seen in `Workout Summaries` <br><br>
![Side profile example](https://github.com/nwisnie/Erg-lytics/blob/main/rowlytics_app/static/images/erg_cool.png)<br><br>

## How to Use Snapshot Library

Users can use `Snapshot Library` to view snapshots of previous workouts they captured. `Capture Workout` Also periodically records 5 second screen recording of workouts. Users are also able to view them here with timestamps of when each recording was captured also included.

## How to Use Workout Summaries

Users can use `Workout Summaries` to view summaries of previous workouts they captured. Each workout entry includes a timestamp, duration, summary, and score when available. For more information on how workout score is calculated, visit https://github.com/nwisnie/Erg-lytics/blob/main/README.md

## How to Use Team View

Users can use `Team View` to view information about their team. To create a team, Enter your team name into the Team Name form and press enter. Coaches on the team can add rowers by entering in their user id in the User ID form. This will automatically add that user to the team. Users can also enter in a team ID to join a team as well.

## How to Login and Signup

To login to Erg-lytics, simply press the login button from the signin page and enter your email and password. You can press the "forgot your password?" button and enter in your email to receieve an email with a code. From there, you can then enter your new password. Creating an account is very similar. Press the sign-up button and enter your email and password. From there, you will recieve an email asking you to confirm your email.
